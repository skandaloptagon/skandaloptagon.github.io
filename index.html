About Me

Having grown up in Atlanta, I am happy to be back as a Masters student at the Georgia Institute of Technology researching information security. Currently I work in the Astrolavos Lab and am advised by Manos Antonakakis. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from Auburn University as well as a co-op diploma.

I also developed a modification for redmine to meet CCI business specifications.
